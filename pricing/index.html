Frequently asked questions

Do I need a payment method to sign up?

No. Free does not require a credit card. A payment method is only needed when you upgrade to a paid plan.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. You can change plans anytime from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What are overages?

If you connect more bank accounts or entities than your plan includes, overage fees may apply. See your plan limits above or contact us for details.

What does MCA reconciliation include?

Our reconciliation team audits your MCA agreements, identifies overcharges and double debits, and works with funders to recover overpayments. Learn more about reconciliation.

Is there a contract or annual commitment?

No. All plans are billed month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
